# Pitch Deck

This document is a template for a pitch deck for the Lisk DApp project.

## Slide 1: Title

*   **Project Name**
*   **Your Name/Team Name**
*   **Tagline**

## Slide 2: Problem

*   **What is the problem you are solving?**
*   **Why is it a problem?**
*   **Who is affected by this problem?**

## Slide 3: Solution

*   **What is your solution?**
*   **How does it solve the problem?**
*   **What are the key features and benefits?**

## Slide 4: Product Demo

*   **Show a demo of your DApp.**
*   **Highlight the key features and user experience.**

## Slide 5: Market Size

*   **How big is the market you are targeting?**
*   **What is the potential for growth?**

## Slide 6: Business Model

*   **How will you make money?**
*   **What is your pricing and revenue model?**

## Slide 7: Team

*   **Who is on your team?**
*   **What is their experience and expertise?**

## Slide 8: Go-to-Market Strategy

*   **How will you acquire users and grow your DApp?**
*   **What are your marketing and sales channels?**

## Slide 9: The Ask

*   **What are you asking for?** (e.g., funding, partnerships, users)
*   **What will you use it for?**
