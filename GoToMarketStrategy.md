# Go-to-Market Strategy

This document outlines the go-to-market strategy for the Lisk DApp project.

## 1. Target Audience

*   **Who are your primary users?** (e.g., DeFi enthusiasts, NFT collectors, developers)
*   **What are their needs and pain points?**
*   **What are their demographics and psychographics?**

## 2. Value Proposition

*   **What is the unique value that your DApp provides?**
*   **How does it solve the problems of your target audience?**
*   **What makes it different from existing solutions?**

## 3. Marketing and Sales Channels

*   **How will you reach your target audience?** (e.g., social media, content marketing, community building, partnerships)
*   **What is your messaging and positioning?**
*   **What is your plan for user acquisition and onboarding?**

## 4. Pricing and Revenue Model

*   **How will your DApp generate revenue?** (e.g., transaction fees, subscription fees, a percentage of NFT sales)
*   **What is your pricing strategy?**

## 5. Key Metrics and KPIs

*   **What are the key metrics you will track to measure the success of your DApp?** (e.g., daily active users, transaction volume, total value locked)
*   **What are your goals for these metrics?**
