# Demo Day Checklist

This document provides a checklist of things to prepare for a demo day.

## 1. Finalize Pitch Deck

*   [ ] Review and refine your pitch deck.
*   [ ] Ensure it is clear, concise, and compelling.
*   [ ] Check for any typos or grammatical errors.

## 2. Prepare Live Demo

*   [ ] Ensure your DApp is fully functional and bug-free.
*   [ ] Prepare a script for your demo, highlighting the key features and user benefits.
*   [ ] Have a backup plan in case of technical difficulties (e.g., a recorded demo).

## 3. Practice Presentation

*   [ ] Rehearse your presentation multiple times.
*   [ ] Time your presentation to ensure it fits within the allotted time.
*   [ ] Get feedback from others and incorporate it into your presentation.

## 4. Prepare for Q&A

*   [ ] Anticipate potential questions from the audience.
*   [ ] Prepare clear and concise answers to these questions.
*   [ ] Be prepared to discuss your technology, business model, and go-to-market strategy in more detail.

## 5. Have a Clear Call to Action

*   [ ] Know what you want to achieve with your presentation.
*   [ ] Have a clear call to action for the audience (e.g., "Visit our website to sign up for the beta," "We are looking for seed funding of $X").
